Section 04: Performance Audit

What's working. What isn't.

Honest current state before the relaunch push. Reference this when briefing new team members or creating your first batch of creator content.

What's Working
  • FinTok is already performing. FinTok / Skinsurance content hitting 12% BE and 5s watch time above benchmark: organically, before paid. This is the single strongest existing signal. Add paid now.
  • 87.8M organic posts on #OlayRetinol24. Unpaid creators are building demand every day. The Retinol24 product story sells itself.
  • Before/after content converts. "7 Night Challenge" format is the highest-converting structure. Creators documenting real results drive the most affiliate revenue.
  • Sensitive skin angle resonates. "No flaking, no purging" is a genuine differentiator vs. RoC and Neutrogena. This is a real USP.
  • Clinical stats are powerful. "97% saw results" and "50% delayed cosmetic procedures" land well in content. These are headline stats.
  • MSC has fresh news. New Triple Collagen Peptide formula (Jan 2026) + GH 2026 Award = credible, timely hook for the next 6 months.
  • SMG partnership drives Millennial cultural relevance. The Nostalgia tribe campaign (Sept '26) has a natural anchor in the celebrity roster.
What Isn't Working
  • Creator network is massively under-scaled. Current: ~34 active creators. Benchmark (Tarte): 200+. At 34, Olay is structurally unable to generate the content volume TikTok Shop demands. This is the #1 fix.
  • Zero LIVE shopping program. LIVE is TikTok Shop's highest-converting format. Competitors run daily LIVE sessions. Olay has none.
  • Tribe-first TikTok strategy is under-served. FinTok, Runner Tok, and Longevity are the three under-served tribes with the most upside. Only FinTok has been tested.
  • "Anti-aging" language pushes younger tribes away. Content should say "skin longevity," "proactive aging," "future-proof." The brand brief says "Aging Gracefully" over "old, but not old": this needs to cascade into all creator briefs.
  • No TikTok-exclusive bundles. Halo heroes, virtual bundles, and 2-packs are called out in the digital playbook but don't exist on TTS yet.
  • AIGC content opportunity untapped. The brand actively wants AI-generated content and Pattern hasn't led here yet. Early mover advantage is available right now.
  • 2-month content lead time rule not being followed. Every tentpole requires 2 months of content lead time for talkability. If you're not building content in August, you're not ready for Prime Big Deal Days in October.

Section 09: Weekly Workflow

How to run this week to week.

The weekly operating cadence. This is how you stay ahead of the 2-month lead time rule and keep creator momentum building.

1
Monday: Content review + creator check-ins
Review last week's affiliate content performance in TikTok Shop Affiliate Center. Flag top 3 videos by GMV and view-to-purchase rate. Check-in with creators who received product but haven't posted. Log in the tracker.
Tool: TTS Affiliate Center → Content Performance tab → filter last 7 days. Export top performers for Spark Ad consideration. Check against tribe alignment for the active campaign month.
2
Tuesday & Thursday: LIVE shopping sessions
Two LIVE sessions per week at launch. Lead with the active campaign's hero product. Use a LIVE-exclusive flash discount code for urgency. Target: 45–75 min per session.
Format: 10 min intro → 20 min demo (before/after) → 15 min Q&A → 10 min close with LIVE-only code. Best time: 7–9pm. Super Serum relaunch month (Aug): every LIVE should feature Super Serum first.
3
Wednesday: Tribe-matched creator outreach
10–15 new creator prospects per week aligned to the active tribe campaign. FinTok month = finance/budget creators. Longevity month = biohacking/wellness creators. Nostalgia = millennial lifestyle creators. Target: 3 new sign-ups per week.
Search terms per tribe: FinTok (#skinsurance #skinvestment) · Longevity (#biohacking #longevity #healthyaging) · Nostalgia (#millennialtok #nostalgiatok) · Runner (#runnertok #marathontraining). Use Creator Marketplace for verified profiles.
4
Friday: Spark Ads + content lead time check
Review Spark Ads performance. Top videos (CTR 2%+, CVR 4%+) get additional budget. Review the 2-month lead time rule: what tentpole is 8 weeks out? Is content already in market to build talkability?
Budget rule: 70% creator gifting/commission · 20% Spark Ads · 10% Shop Ads. Tentpole check: if the next tentpole is 8 weeks away and content isn't live yet, raise a flag immediately.
5
Friday: Weekly report
Total GMV, active creator count, top-performing video, affiliate CVR, LIVE GMV, Spark Ad ROAS. Compare against weekly targets. Flag anomalies. Note which tribe content is outperforming: feed back into next week's briefs.
Insight loop: what angle worked this week → brief the next 10 creators on it. TikTok value = volume + instant wins that replicate at scale. The feedback loop from affiliates is Olay's fastest signal.
